## Chapter: Chapter 7 – Alternating Current

### ID 258

```latex
\begin{aligned}
i&=i_{m}\sin\!\left(\omega t+\frac{\pi}{2}\right),\\[4pt]
i_{m}&=\frac{v_{m}}{X_{C}},\\[4pt]
X_{C}&=\frac{1}{\omega C},
\end{aligned}
```

where \(X_C\) is called the **capacitive reactance**.
